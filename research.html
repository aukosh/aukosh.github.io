My research lies at the interface of probability theory, high-dimensional statistics, optimization, and mathematical physics. I am particularly interested in the mathematical foundations of data science, including high-dimensional learning dynamics, statistical-computational tradeoffs, random optimization, and spin glass methods.

  • Probability theory, mathematical foundations of data science, mathematical physics
  • High-dimensional learning, statistical-computational tradeoffs, spin glasses
